Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

tallydrop is a simple expense tracking app.

tallydrop does not require an account and does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Your tallies, drops, notes, and imported data are stored locally on your device. tallydrop does not operate a server for syncing or storing your expense data.

tallydrop may request access to your photo library only when you choose to save exported receipt images. This access is used only for that action.

tallydrop supports importing and exporting JSON, CSV, and image files. These actions are initiated by you, and the exported files are handled by iOS sharing or saving features.

tallydrop does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking.

If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be removed from your device according to iOS behavior.
